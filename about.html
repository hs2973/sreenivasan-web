We use several neuroimaging techniques to gain insight into how working memory and other elements of goal-directed behavior are implemented in the human brain.

Some questions that we are interested in:

  • What are the biological constraints on the amount and/or resolution of information held in working memory?
  • How is information endcoded and maintained such that it can persist in the presence of distracting stimuli?
  • How does working memory modulate our perception of the world?
